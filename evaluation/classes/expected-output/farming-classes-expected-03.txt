Class: farmer
- original text: A farmer is an individual who engages in the activity of agriculture, growing and harvesting crops


Class: crop
- original text: Crops are plants grown in large amounts and cultivated by farmers for profit or subsistence, such as cereals, oil seeds, vegetables, and fruits.


Class: cereal
- original text: Cereals are plants cultivated for their edible grain.


Class: seed plant
- original text: Seed plants are grown for oil production from seeds, significant for both food and industrial uses.


Class: vegetable
- original text: Vegetables are plants cultivated for their parts consumable by humans or other animals as food.


Class: fruit
- original text: Fruits are plants cultivated for producing their seed-associated fleshy structures that are typically sweet or sour and edible in the raw state such as apples, bananas, grapes, lemons, oranges, and strawberries


Class: machinery
- original text: Machinery encompasses mechanical tools and equipment used in farming, crucial for tasks such as plowing, seeding, spraying, and harvesting.


Class: tractor
- original text: A tractor is a versatile piece of machinery essential for multiple farming tasks, from soil preparation to crop maintenance.


Class: harvester
- original text: A harvester includes machinery that combines harvesting tasks such as reaping, threshing, and winnowing into a single operation.


Class: planter
- original text: A planter is machinery designed to plant seeds efficiently and accurately, crucial for optimal crop distribution and growth.


Class: sprayer
- original text: A sprayer is used for the precise application of liquids like pesticides and fertilizers, vital for crop protection and growth enhancement.


Class: harvest
- original text: Harvest is the production obtained from crops, quantified and evaluated in terms of quantity and quality, representing the final output of the agricultural cycle.


Class: field
- original text: Fields are areas of land used for growing crops, Fields can be but does not have to be surrounded by a fence.


Class: planting
- original text: Planting represents the agricultural activity of planting crops in a specific field during a specific year, capturing the details of the planting process.
